Brute Force Seating Arrangement Example

Students: A, B, C

Rules:
1. Friends should not sit next to each other.
2. Students from the same city should not sit next to each other.

Possible Seating Arrangements:

1. ABC
2. ACB
3. BAC
4. BCA
5. CAB
6. CBA

Each arrangement is checked to see whether any two neighboring students
break the rules.

Example Check:

Arrangement: ABC
Check A-B → Are they friends or from same city?
Check B-C → Are they friends or from same city?

If no rule is broken, the seating arrangement is valid.
Otherwise, it is rejected.
